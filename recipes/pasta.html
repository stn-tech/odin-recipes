The ingredients

  • lb penne pasta
  • tbsp butter
  • 2 tsp minced garlic
  • 3 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup chicken broth

The recipe

  1. Cook Pasta. Begin by cooking the pasta according to the package instructions (we used penne).
  2. Make Sauce. While pasta is cooking, melt butter in a medium sauce pan, add garlic and cook for 1 minute over medium heat
  3. Add flour and cook for an addition minute, stirring constantly
  4. Add milk and broth, stirring constantly
  5. Cook until sauce boils and thickens
  6. Add parsley and parmesan cheese, salt and pepper
  7. Stir until cheese has melted
  8. Combine. Mix in pasta and serve immediately

Enjoy!
