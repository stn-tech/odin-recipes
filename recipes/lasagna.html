Delicious lasagna

Return to main page

The ingredients

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 pound ground beef*
  • 1 onion, diced
  • Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste

The recipe

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. Lightly oil a 9 by 13 baking dish or coat with nonstick spray
  3. In a large pot of boiling salted water, cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions
  4. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium high heat. Add ground beef and onion and cook until beef has browned, about 3-5 minutes, making sure to crumble the beef as it cooks; season with salt and pepper, to taste. Drain excess fat. Stir in tomatoes and Italian seasoning until well combined
  5. In a medium bowl, combine ricotta, 1/2 cup mozzarella and egg; set aside
  6. Spread 1 cup tomato mixture onto the bottom of a 9 by 13 baking dish; top with 3 lasagna noodles, 1/2 of the ricotta cheese mixture and 1 cup mozzarella cheese. Repeat with a second layer. Top with remaining noodles, tomato mixture, 1 cup mozzarella cheese and Parmesan.
  7. Place into oven and bake for 35-45 minutes, or until bubbling. Then broil for 2-3 minutes, or until top is browned in spots
  8. Let cool 15 minutes. Serve, garnished with parsley, if desired

Enjoy!
